For the ML enthusiasts

CPU optimizations have physical limits. For ultimate parallelization (thousands of cores) or autograd, we move to GPUs.

PyTorch

Wrap your model in torch.compile() for ~2x JIT speedup automatically.

CuPy

Drop-in NumPy replacement: import cupy as cp yields instant GPU array execution.

JAX

XLA compilation, auto-vectorization (vmap), and hyper-fast auto-grad.

Optimization Tree

  • Standard Math? NumPy / SciPy
  • Custom Heavy Loop? Numba JIT
  • Independent Tasks/I-O? Joblib
  • Bigger than RAM? Dask
  • Working with GPUs? CuPy + JAX

Take Home Messages

Vectorize (NumPy)

Pre-compiled C routines and SIMD hardware instructions should be your first reflex.

JIT Compile (Numba)

Add @njit to custom state-dependent loops to achieve near C++ speeds instantly.

Distribute (Joblib)

Bypass the GIL entirely and spread independent tasks/loops across all CPU cores.

Scale out (Dask)

Map operations lazily into task graphs and stream chunks when exceeding machine RAM.
